===========================================================
 Best Practices in QM/MM Simulation of Biomolecular Systems
===========================================================

This guide provides researchers, especially those new to QM/MM
simulation of biomolecular systems, with critical advice on best
practices considered to be important by the organisers and speakers of
the `BioExcel Virtual Workshop on Best Practices in QM/MM Simulation
of Biomolecular Systems
<https://bioexcel.eu/events/virtual-workshop-best-practices-in-qm-mm-simulation-of-biomolecular-systems/>`_.
